ABOUT

V Connect Global Experts (VCGE)

Embark on a transformative journey in global trade and supply chain excellence with V Connect Global Experts (VCGE). Established in 2008, VCGE has been a beacon of innovation, specializing in international trade encompassing Import & Export, meticulous clearing & forwarding, and seamless warehousing and distribution services.


Our Expertise:

When it comes to global trade and supply chain management, VCGE stands out as a leader.
Our focus spans across international trade operations, including Import & Export, clearing & forwarding,
and comprehensive warehousing and distribution services. From the initial setup of the organization
with meticulous documentation to hassle-free Import and Export processes, packing of
finished products, and reaching end customers, we provide end-to-end solutions.


Our expertise covers a wide array of services:
  • Import Exports licenses, ICEGATE Registration, AD Code Registration, and comprehensive Import and Export business documentations and registrations compliant with ever-changing regulations.
  • Trade Promotion Council membership registrations and certifications, along with international business-related documentations.
  • LUT and Advance Authorization for Imports without tax.
  • Licenses for restricted exports in line with current norms.
  • Destination-specific registrations and compliance certifications across the globe.
  • Product inspections and quality control certifications.
  • Extensive affiliations with industry bodies such as MSME, GST, IEC, DGFT, ICEGATE, FSSAI, and many more.
